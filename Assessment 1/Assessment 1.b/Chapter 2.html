Plot



How to train your dragons

A norse teenager named Hiccup the son of Stoic the Vast from the island called Berk. Where in this island, their way of life is slaying dragons. He always have a different humor and views than the other villagers that makes him an outcasts, despite being the son of the chief. He was then put into a slaying dragon school, in order to prove himself. But it all changes when he befriended a dragon named Toothless, now he have the chance to change the people's future.
